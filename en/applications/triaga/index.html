Triaga

Triaga sends medication reminders on schedule, tracks whether each dose was taken, and keeps everything private on your device. Details and answers below; direct contact at the bottom.

What Triaga does

Triaga is a medication reminder app built around one idea: your health schedule belongs to you, not to a server. Set a reminder once — for a single dose, specific days of the week, or a repeating interval like every 8 hours — and Triaga notifies you at the right time, even without an internet connection. When a reminder comes due, mark it Taken, Skipped, or snooze it for later, and every dose is logged to a clear history you can review or share.

Features

  • Flexible scheduling — once, on selected days, or repeating at an interval.
  • Reliable offline notifications — exact-alarm scheduling keeps timing accurate, no internet required.
  • Critical Alerts — let a reminder break through Do Not Disturb; a safety feature included free for every user. Sound still depends on your phone's own ringer and volume.
  • Dose history — a clear Taken / Skipped / Missed record for every reminder.
  • Backup & Restore — manually export your data to a file and restore it on a new device, no cloud account required.
  • PDF sharing — share a dose history report with a doctor or caregiver (Premium).
  • Light, Dark, Obsidian, and Bubblegum themes — Obsidian (true-black) and Bubblegum (pink and playful) are Premium options.
  • Available in English, Spanish, and Portuguese, following your device's language automatically.

Free and Premium

The free plan is a complete tool, not a trial: track up to 3 medications, your full history in the calendar, and every safety feature, including Critical Alerts. Triaga Premium removes the reminder limit and unlocks PDF sharing (up to 90 days per report) and the Obsidian and Bubblegum themes. Premium is billed and managed entirely through Google Play, and can be viewed, changed, or cancelled at any time from your Google Play subscription settings — see the Terms of Use for full billing and cancellation details.

FAQ

How do reminders work?

Add a medication and choose a schedule — once, on specific days of the week, or repeating at an interval. Triaga sends a local notification at each scheduled time — no internet connection required.

I'm not receiving notifications. What should I check?

Confirm notifications are enabled for Triaga in your device settings, and that battery optimization or "Do Not Disturb" rules aren't silencing the app. If Do Not Disturb is blocking reminders, enable Critical Alerts from Settings so they can break through it — note that this doesn't override your phone's own ringer, so if your ringer or volume is set to silent or vibrate, the notification will still arrive without sound. Reinstalling the app will not restore reminders unless you re-enter your schedule or restore from a backup.

Can I edit or delete a medication schedule?

Yes. Open the medication from the main list to edit its times, dosage notes, or delete it entirely.

What happens if I miss a dose?

Each dose is marked Taken, Skipped, or Missed and kept in your history. Triaga will show a reminder as pending until you act on it. Triaga does not diagnose, prescribe, or offer medical advice — always follow guidance from your doctor or pharmacist.

What is Critical Alerts?

Critical Alerts lets a reminder break through Do Not Disturb, so a real dose isn't blocked by that mode alone. It's a safety feature, included free for every user — turn it on from Settings. Note it does not override your phone's own ringer: if your ringer or volume is set to silent or vibrate, the notification will still arrive, but it won't make a sound.

Is Triaga free?

Yes — the free plan tracks up to 3 medications, your full history in the calendar, and includes Critical Alerts. Triaga Premium removes the reminder limit and adds PDF sharing and the Obsidian and Bubblegum themes, billed through Google Play. See "Free and Premium" above for details.

What languages does Triaga support?

English, Spanish, and Portuguese — Triaga follows your device's language automatically.

Do I need an account?

No. Triaga has no login and does not sync data anywhere. Everything lives on your device. See the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use for details.

How do I delete my data, or move to a new phone?

Uninstalling Triaga removes all locally stored schedules and reminders from your device. If you're switching devices, use Backup & Restore in Settings first to save a file you can restore afterward — otherwise the data is not recoverable.
