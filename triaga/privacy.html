Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026. This policy covers the Triaga mobile application only.

Summary

Triaga is designed to work entirely on your device. It does not require an account, does not sync to a server, and does not share your information with third parties. This page explains what that means in detail.

Information we collect

Triaga does not collect personal data on our servers. Medication names, dosages, schedules, and reminder history are stored locally on your device only. Jayzil Studios has no access to this information and cannot view, retrieve, or recover it.

Accounts and sign-in

Triaga does not require or offer account creation. There is no login, and no personal identifiers (such as name or email) are collected through the app.

Cloud sync and backups

Triaga does not sync data to Jayzil Studios or any third-party cloud service. If your device's operating system includes its own backup feature (such as a system-level cloud backup), that backup is managed by your device manufacturer, not by Jayzil Studios.

Third parties and analytics

Triaga does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Permissions

Triaga requests notification permission so it can alert you at scheduled medication times. This permission is used solely for that purpose.

Data deletion

Because all data is stored locally, uninstalling the app permanently deletes all Triaga data from your device.

Children's privacy

Triaga is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from children, as it does not collect data at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
