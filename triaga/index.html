Triaga Support

Triaga sends medication reminders on schedule — nothing more. Answers below; direct contact at the bottom.

What Triaga does

Triaga is a medication reminder app. Set a schedule once, and Triaga notifies you when it's time to take each dose. That's the entire job — no accounts, no social features, no clutter.

FAQ

How do reminders work?

Add a medication and a schedule inside the app. Triaga sends a local notification at each scheduled time — no internet connection required.

I'm not receiving notifications. What should I check?

Confirm notifications are enabled for Triaga in your device settings, and that battery optimization or "Do Not Disturb" rules aren't silencing the app. Reinstalling the app will not restore reminders unless you re-enter your schedule.

Can I edit or delete a medication schedule?

Yes. Open the medication from the main list to edit its times, dosage notes, or delete it entirely.

What happens if I miss a dose?

Triaga will show the missed reminder until you dismiss or mark it as taken. Triaga does not diagnose, prescribe, or offer medical advice — always follow guidance from your doctor or pharmacist.

Do I need an account?

No. Triaga has no login and does not sync data anywhere. Everything lives on your device. See the privacy policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

Uninstalling Triaga removes all locally stored schedules and reminders from your device.
